MLS 2024 Ticket-Revenue Analysis

Estimated each MLS club's 2024 home-gate revenue by scraping attendance from FBref and merging with average ticket-price data. Reveals the Messi attendance uplift and revenue leaders.

Python pandas seaborn Jupyter

Overview

Goal: estimate each Major League Soccer club's 2024 home-gate revenue by scraping official attendance from FBref and merging it with average ticket-price data (Team Marketing Report & TicketIQ). The output—a consolidated .csv, interactive charts, and a Python notebook—lets stakeholders benchmark fan demand and pricing strategy across the league.

Downloads

Schedule + Attendance CSV Ticket Price Table CSV Jupyter Notebook View Notebook HTML

Methodology

  1. Scrape 2024 match-by-match attendance from FBref using pandas.read_html.
  2. Acquire average face-value ticket prices per club (Team Marketing Report Fan Cost Index) plus TicketIQ secondary-market averages for validation.
  3. Merge attendance & price on club name, compute game-level gate revenue, then groupby("Home") for club totals.
  4. Visualize with Seaborn: revenue leaderboard, box-plots, week-over-week trend.
  5. Share notebook & datasets so others can plug in updated ticket prices or simulate "what-if" scenarios.

Key Findings

  • Inter Miami led the league with an estimated $70.3 million in home-gate revenue—3.1× the league-average club.
  • Median MLS club pulled ≈ $22 million, driven by average ticket price of $65 and ~21,000 fans/game.
  • Attendance uplift of +27% on matches when Lionel Messi played compared with the league baseline.
  • Week-over-week trend shows record highs around Rivalry Week and Leagues Cup break, dipping mid-summer outside marquee markets.
